Stuffed Potatoes

Veggie stuffed potatoes with mediterranean toppings!

Ingredients

Potato
  • Potatoes - regular or sweet
  • Baked beans or refried beans
  • Brown or red onion
  • Spring onion
  • Tomato
  • herbs: coriander, Basil, Mint, Chilli, garlic
  • Cheese
Sauce
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon Juice

Method

  1. Bake Potatoes until cooked through
  2. remove potatoes from oven, cut in half or quarters and add a teaspoon of butter
  3. Allow butter to melt, Add in hot bean mixture
