Stuffed Potatoes

Veggie stuffed potatoes with mediterranean toppings!

Ingredients

Potato
  • Potatoes - regular or sweet
  • Baked beans or refried beans
  • Brown or red onion
  • Spring onion
  • Tomato
  • Herbs: coriander, Basil, Mint, Chilli, Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste or salsa
Sauce
  • Greek yogurt
  • Salt & pepper
  • Lemon Juice

Method

  1. Bake Potatoes in oven at 180 deg C until cooked through
  2. Finley chop onion garlic and chilli, add to a pot with olive oil to brown
  3. Once spice mix is aromatic (~2mins), add in chosen bean mix
  4. stir bean mix with spices for 15m, until heated through, simmer and season with salt an pepper
  5. Mix greek yogurt, salt, pepper, lemon juice and chopped mint in a small container to create a topping sauce
  6. Remove potatoes from oven, cut in half or quarters and add a teaspoon of butter
  7. Allow butter to melt, Add in hot bean mixture
  8. Immediately add cheese to top of hot potato and bean mix to ensure it melts
  9. Add chopped tomato, and spring onion
  10. Drisle with Yogurt sauce and top with more chopped basil and coriander
  11. top with Hot Sauce, Cheese and salt and pepper as desired
